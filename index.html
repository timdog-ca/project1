about

I am a passionate maker who enjoys working with others bring ideas to life. The desire to make and design started at a young age with my parents. My mother was always doing crafts and creating which drove we interest in design and father admin clerk in the military which drove my interest in stationery and paperwork.

It was not until high school where I got to use my first MacIntosh computer and fell in love with the possibilities for digital design. I connected with one of the teachers to help out with a school science newsletter. After high school I got my first job working for a printing & publishing company where I worked in the pre-press department doing manual newspaper layout. Within the first year, I was moved to the new digital layout department.
